Work Groupings

    Deep Cleaning

  • Deep cleaning typically takes longer than a regular cleaning, especially if you schedule a deep cleaning for your entire home. It involves lots of detail work, which takes more effort.
    1. Cleaning under furniture
    2. Wiping down ceiling fans and light fixtures
    3. Cleaning blinds
    4. Dusting individual decorations
    5. Sanitizing surfaces
    6. Dusting lamp shades
    7. Cleaning baseboards, window frames, and door frames
    8. Cleaning inside window sills and window tracks
    9. Cleaning behind and under appliances
    10. Cleaning inside appliances, including your oven, refrigerator, and microwave

    Regular Cleaning

  • Regular cleaning usually happens on a weekly basis. The goal is to keep up with basic cleaning tasks.
    Typical tasks that happen during this service include:
    1. Tidying up your spaces
    2. Cleaning floors
    3. Wiping surfaces in kitchens and bathrooms
    4. Taking out the trash
    5. General dusting
